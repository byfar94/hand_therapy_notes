# Posterior Elbow Orthosis

## outline

Measure the upper arm circumference
Measure the DPC to Middle upper arm

Material width: slightly small the circumference of the upper arm
Material Length: DPC to middle of the upper arm

## material

aquaplast

## application

Start at mid upper arm and pinch splinting material around the arm

Then stretch material gently to be flush to the posterior arm

Then pinch around the DPC crease distal to the thumb and pinch around the wrist

Then pinch directly over the elbow

Then fill in where needed

## strapping

Simple:
Two strap on the upper arm
three strap on the lower arm, one begin distal to the thumb

## Edges

foam/ padding over teh DPC and the upper arm sections
